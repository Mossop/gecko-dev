What's new on flowstate

May 2, 2022

Stay focused on your active view
With just a single active state, it’s easier to stay in the flow on your current view. We’ve removed the two active states to simplify the experience.
Event cards are more accessible for screen readers
Flowstate no longer exposes the icons on event cards as undescribed images to screen readers.
Stay on task with improved notifications
Receive only the system notifications you need. You’ll no longer receive meeting event notifications from disconnected services.
